INTEL 1: THE BROTHER

This handsome man is Yuri Briar. He is Yor Briar's younger brother. He works at the SSS (State Security Service). There are rumors going around that he has a sister-complex..

INTEL 3: THE LITTLE BRAT

This little brat is the second son of Donovan Desmond. He attends Eden Academy as part of Cecile Hall, Group 3. He's been seen bullying Anya Forger, but people are suspecting he actually has feelings for her.
